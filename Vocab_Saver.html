ğŸ“‹ Privacy Policy Overview

Welcome to AI Vocabulary Treasure (hereinafter referred to as "this extension" or "we"). We understand the importance of privacy to you, and therefore have established this privacy policy to explain how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

Important Notice: This extension is committed to putting user privacy protection first. All vocabulary data is stored on your local device. We do not collect, upload, or share your learning data.

1. Information Collection and Use

1.1 Information We Collect

This extension may collect the following types of information:

  • Vocabulary Data: Words, definitions, categories, and other learning content you save (completely stored locally)
  • Learning Records: Learning progress, study time, and other statistical data (completely stored locally)
  • User Settings: Interface language, theme mode, category settings, and other preference configurations (completely stored locally)
  • Login Information: If you choose to log in to the Hengchuang AI Tools platform, we will store your login token for authentication
  • Permission Information: Verification information for extension usage permissions

1.2 Purpose of Information Use

  • Provide vocabulary management and learning features
  • Save your learning progress and preference settings
  • Verify user identity and usage permissions
  • Improve extension features and user experience

2. Data Storage and Security

2.1 Local Storage

Core Commitment: All your vocabulary data, learning records, and personal settings are stored on your local device through Chrome's chrome.storage API and will not be uploaded to any server.

2.2 Data Security Measures

  • Use Chrome's official storage API to ensure data security
  • Data is only stored and processed on your device
  • Support data export function, you can back up your data at any time
  • Local data will be automatically cleared when the extension is uninstalled

3. Permission Descriptions

This extension requires the following Chrome browser permissions to provide full functionality:

3.1 storage

Purpose: Store your vocabulary data, learning records, and settings locally.

Note: This is a necessary permission for the core functionality of the extension. All data is stored only on your device.

3.2 contextMenus

Purpose: Quickly add vocabulary through the right-click menu after selecting text on a webpage.

Note: Only triggered when you actively use the right-click menu function.

3.3 activeTab

Purpose: Get the text content you selected on the current webpage for adding vocabulary.

Note: Only accesses current tab content when you actively use the add vocabulary function.

3.4 tabs

Purpose: Open a new tab to access the Hengchuang AI Tools platform for login or purchasing permissions.

Note: Only used when you click the login or purchase button.

3.5 Host Permissions (<all_urls>)

Purpose: Allow you to use vocabulary collection features on any webpage.

Note: This is the standard permission declaration format required by the Chrome Web Store. In practice, the extension only accesses webpage content when you actively use it, and only retrieves the text you select.

3.6 Specific Domain Permission (tool.hcceo.com)

Purpose: Communicate with the Hengchuang AI Tools platform for user login verification and permission checking.

Note: Only used for identity authentication and permission management, does not involve vocabulary data transmission.

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 AI Translation Services (Optional)

If you choose to configure AI translation features, the extension will call the third-party AI service API you select:

  • Google Translate
  • OpenAI (ChatGPT)
  • Google Gemini
  • DeepSeek AI
  • Grok AI
Important Notes:
  • Your API keys are stored locally and will not be sent to our servers
  • Translation requests are sent directly to your chosen AI service provider
  • Please refer to each AI service provider's privacy policy for details
  • Using AI services may incur costs, please check each provider's pricing information

4.2 Hengchuang AI Tools Platform

Used for user login verification and permission management:

  • Platform URL:http://tool.hcceo.com
  • Purpose: User authentication, permission verification
  • Data Transmission: Only transmits login tokens and permission verification information, does not involve vocabulary data

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Promise:

  • We will not sell, rent, or trade your personal information
  • We will not share your vocabulary learning data with third parties
  • We will not disclose your information unless required by law or with your explicit consent

6. User Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access Right: View all your stored vocabulary and learning data at any time
  • Export Right: Export data in CSV or TXT format
  • Deletion Right: Delete individual vocabulary items or clear all data
  • Uninstall Right: All local data will be cleared when the extension is uninstalled

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

This extension does not use cookies or any tracking technologies to collect user behavior data.

8. Children's Privacy Protection

This extension is suitable for users of all ages. We do not actively collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child has provided us with personal information without permission, please contact us.

9. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated policy will be posted on this page and the "Last Updated" date will be updated. For significant changes, we will notify you through in-extension notifications.
